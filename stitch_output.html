What started as a single red Peterbilt in 1994 has evolved into a premier regional logistics powerhouse. Frasier Transport was founded on the belief that a driver's handshake should be as reliable as their engine.

Thirty years later, we remain family-owned and operated, ensuring that every load we carry receives the same personal dedication that built this company from the ground up.
